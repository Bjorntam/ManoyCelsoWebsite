ABOUT

Manoy Celso Pancit Bato

Manoy Celso Authentic Bato is a specialty store renowned for its Pancit Bato, a traditional noodle dish from Bato, Camarines Sur in the Bicol region of the Philippines. This store prides itself on preserving the authentic preparation methods, including sun-drying the noodles, which contributes to their distinct texture and flavor. The menu features a variety of Pancit Bato dishes, each reflecting the rich culinary heritage of Bicol. Manoy Celso's commitment to authenticity makes it a beloved destination for those seeking genuine Filipino cuisine.

Arcel Celso “Manoy”

Arcel Celso or "manoy" Celso is a notable figure in the Filipino culinary scene, particularly known for his dedication to preserving and promoting the traditional dish, Pancit Bato. Originating from Bato, Camarines Sur in the Bicol region, Pancit Bato is a unique type of noodle that is sun-dried, giving it a distinct texture and flavor that sets it apart from other noodle dishes.
